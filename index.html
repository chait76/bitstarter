This product makes use of meteorological & historical data and carries out predictions for the likely power generation from renewable sources(Solar, Wind, Small Hydro). This prediction could be made for day ahead production and also for fortnightly, monthly and quarterly future periods. This predictive knowledge could be used by power plant operators to schedule more accurately the power injection into the grid and avoid likely penalty from the grid operator. It could be used by utilities to plan their energy- procurement mix and buy in advance the RECs. It could also be used by electricity traders to price electricity in energy exchange.

The use of this product would help plant owners avoid hefty penalties levied by the regulator in case of deviation from the earlier declared power injection schedule into the grid. It would also lead to greater integration of renewable energy into electricity grid and bring about stability in grid operations due to better predicatbility in generation. This would also lead to a spurt in trading of REC (Renewable energy certificates) which would fetch additional revenue for plant owners and the traders. Greater predicatbility for renewable energy generation would lead to further acceptability and adoption of 'Distributed Generation' and would give a filip to industrial activity in villages and remote areas of developing countries. This would help avoid burning of costly and polluting fossil fuel. It would also help slacken the demand for the commodities like coal, oil and gas leading to attendant correction in commodity prices.
